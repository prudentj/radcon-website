Letter from the Chair

transmission_from_chair.txt

Hello, all my friends from across the universe! We hope your travels here were easy and pleasant.

This year we have put together an event that we hope brings a great and happy time for you and yours. We brought back many fan favorites and some new and exciting things with an eye for both young and old.

A few things to remember while you look around: we have many friends, so be thoughtful. We all have things that make us happy and things that bug us, try not to be the bug. Eat a couple meals, wash up when you can, and sleep when you can. We all need breaks.

Remember, we (staffers) are all volunteers and we are not omniscient, but we are here to help.

How to Enter

Sign up must be completed on Saturday by 1:00 PM. Winners announced following the Masquerade. Registering grants RadCon permission to use images for promotional purposes.

Skill Divisions

Youth (12 & Under)

Children's Masquerade. Adult supervision required.

Novice

Little to no experience, first time performers, never won an award.

Apprentice

Some experience, entered before, may or may not have won.

Journeyman

Significant experience, two "Best of Novice" awards, or "Best of Show" at smaller cons.

Master

Extensive experience, professional actors, or persons who make costumes professionally.

General Rules

  • Content limited to PG-13

  • NO NUDITY - real or implied
  • No pyrotechnics or projectiles
  • No live animals (except service animals)
  • No messy substances
  • All electric power must be self-contained
  • Special effects must be pre-approved
  • Presentations limited to one minute
